Support – RobloxCrashMonitor

If you need help, encounter a bug, or experience unexpected behavior, you can reach out using:

• GitHub Issues: Recommended for bugs, errors, or crashes
• Discord: snowwhitecodez

Before requesting support, please include:
• Your Windows version
• Whether you are using the .exe or Python script
• A copy of your config.json (remove webhook before sharing)
• Any crash logs (optional)
• Steps to reproduce the issue

This information helps diagnose and resolve the problem more quickly.
